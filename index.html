How to Play The Classic Frogger

About the Game:

In this game you have a Player and Enemies (Bugs). The goal of the player is to reach the water, without colliding into any one of the enemies. Once the player collides with an enemy, the game is reset and the player moves back to the start square. Once the player reaches the water the game is won you are moved to the next level.

Game Control Keys:

The player can move with left, right, up and down key on your keyboard.
